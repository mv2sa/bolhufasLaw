Services

Deportation Defense

Our office represents individual facing removal proceedings. We will explore any applicable defenses to removal, including but not limited to:

  • Bond Hearings
  • Requests for Release Under Post Order Custody Review (POCR)
  • Asylum, Withholding of Removal and/or Relief under the Convention Against Torture
  • Citizenship/Naturalization During Removal Proceedings
  • Cancellation of Removal for a Lawful Permanent Resident
  • Cancellation of Removal for Certain Non-Residents
  • Special Rule Cancellation of Removal under NACARA
  • Violence Against Women’s Act (VAWA) Cancelation of Removal
  • VAWA Adjustment of Status
  • Adjustment of Status
  • Adjustment of Status under INA § 245(i) Voluntary Departure
  • Waivers:
    • Relief Under INA § 212(h) (certain crimes)
    • Relief Under INA § 237(a)(1)(h) (fraud)
    • Relief Under INA § 212(k) (no visa or visa was not properly issued)
    • Relief Under INA § 212(i) (fraud)
    • Relief Under INA § 212(a)(9)(B)(v) (unlawful presence)
  • Removal of Green Card Conditions (I-751)
  • Motions to Terminate, Challenging Evidence and/or Grounds of Deportability
  • T Nonimmigrant Visa: Trafficking Victims Protection Act of 2000
  • S Nonimmigrant Visa: Informant Visa
  • U Nonimmigrant Visa: Victim of Certain Qualifying Crimes
  • SIJS Special Immigrant Juvenile Status
  • TPS: Temporary Protected Status
  • Registry
  • Private Bills
  • Prosecutorial Discretion
  • Deferred Action for Childhood Arrivals (DACA)
  • Relief Under Former INA § 212(c)
