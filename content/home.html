Comprehensive Immigration Legal Services

Services

At Santanna Law Offices, we represent detained and non-detained individuals in diverse immigration matters including citizenship, asylum, withholding of removal, relief under the Convention Against Torture (CAT), cancellation of removal, adjustment of status, U nonimmigrant visas, VAWA, deferred action for childhood Arrivals (DACA), bond, termination of proceedings and prosecutorial discretion.
We take pride in finding solutions to complex immigration cases and we go above and beyond to achieve positive results for our clients.
If you or a loved one is in removal proceedings or would like to explore eligibility for any immigration benefits, contact us for a legal consultation.
We speak Spanish and Portuguese.
